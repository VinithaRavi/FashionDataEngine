Overview

This sweater merges the silhouette of our iconic Polo shirt with lightweight, easy-care cashmere.
Style Number: 411910

Details

  • Regular Fit: a slightly trim silhouette; 2" wider at the chest than our Slim Fit.
  • Size medium has a 27½" body length, an 18" shoulder, a 20½" chest, and a 25" sleeve length. Sleeve length changes ½" between sizes.
  • Rib-knit Polo collar with faux-suede piping at the interior back of the neck. Three-button placket. Genuine mother-of-pearl buttons.
  • Long sleeves with rib-knit cuffs.
  • Rib-knit hem.
  • Signature embroidered pony at the left chest.
  • 100% cashmere.
  • Machine washable. Imported.
  • Model is 6'1"/185 cm and wears a size medium.
